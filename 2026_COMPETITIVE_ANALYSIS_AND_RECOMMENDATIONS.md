## Executive Summary

After comprehensive research of post-October 2025 announcements, the enterprise automation and AI coding market is converging on **"Agentic Automation"** as the defining paradigm for 2026. Three key trends are emerging:

1. **Agentic AI Explosion:** 40% of enterprise apps will include AI agents by end of 2026 (Gartner)
2. **Autonomous Enterprise:** Up to 80% of work will be automated or AI-assisted (Automation Anywhere + Aisera vision)
3. **Process Reasoning Over LLMs:** Moving beyond simple language models to context-aware process engines

**Market Size:** The autonomous agents market will grow from $4.35B (2025) to $103.28B by 2034 (42% CAGR).

**AGI Workforce Position:**
- ✅ **Strengths:** We have 85% of the technical foundation competitors are building toward
- ✅ **Architecture:** We already have parallel execution, caching, and multi-LLM routing
- ⚠️ **Gaps:** Missing enterprise-specific features competitors announced for 2026
- ⚠️ **Risk:** Competitors are spending $200M+ on acquisitions and R&D
